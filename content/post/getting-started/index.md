## Q&A on **infinity**

1. What is infinity?
+ Infinity is a number, a special one. It is the "highest" number of all little numbers. 

2. Why do we need it?
+ Because numbers keep going bigger and bigger and BIGGER to infinity.

3. How do we use it?
